# 盯鞋乡村 · 德克萨斯的噪音墙

> **风格：** 美国南方乡村音乐 (Country) 缝合 盯鞋摇滚 (Shoegaze)
> **配器：** 钢棒吉他 (Pedal Steel) + 巨大反向混响 + 法兹效果 · 慵懒牛仔嗓音
> **节奏：** 80 BPM · G大调
> **时长：** 待定

---

## 意象

被龙卷风撕碎的谷仓
融化的沙漠地平线
浸泡在威士忌里的旧照片

---

## 结构说明

钢棒吉他原本极其凄美的滑音，
在巨大混响的加持下，
变成了一道道悬浮在空中的巨大光晕，
彻底颠覆乡村音乐"小酒馆"的听觉格局。

---

## 生成提示词

American Southern Country meets Shoegaze: Pedal Steel Guitar with massive 
reverse reverb and fuzz effects creating tsunami-like psychedelic noise wall, 
lazy cowboy vocal delivery buried deep in the mix, 80 BPM G major, lazy 
drone-like atmosphere. Imagery: tornado-shattered barns, melting desert 
horizon, old photos soaked in whiskey, endless Texas plains under violent 
stormy skies. The pedal steel's mournful slide notes become suspended 
halos of light in huge reverb tails, creating epic vast loneliness 
feeling, wild west狂风骤雨中闭上眼睛的意境。
