I wanted a blog that is fast, easy to maintain, and pleasant to write for.

Astro gives me static output by default, so pages load quickly and hosting stays cheap.
Markdown keeps the writing workflow simple and durable.

The plan is to publish short, practical posts every week and keep the design intentional but lightweight.
